NYC MAYOR'S MANAGEMENT REPORT — 311 CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER (FY2024)
Source: Mayor's Management Report, Fiscal Year 2024, pp. 169-174
Office of Technology and Innovation | Deputy Commissioner: Joe Morrisroe

============================================================
TOTAL CONTACTS
============================================================

- FY2024: 38.2 million total contacts across call center, website, mobile, text, and social media.
- 18.2 million contacts via 311's website — a 21% increase over FY2023.
- 17.5 million calls received, plus 2.5 million contacts via mobile app, text, and social media.
- Calls in languages other than English or Spanish increased 21% year over year.

============================================================
AGENCY RESOURCES
============================================================

- Expenditures: FY24: $61.9M (plan: $56.7M; 5-year trend: UP)

============================================================
EQUITY FOCUS
============================================================

- Services available via phone in 175 languages, 24/7/365.
- Expanded automated messaging to 10 designated citywide languages.
